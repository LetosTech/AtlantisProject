Donate

There are several ways to make a financial contribution to The Katie Lynch Foundation. We appreciate all donations! Thank you for your support.

  • Direct Donations
  • Charity Runners Raffle
  • To Donate By Credit Card
  • Official Race Sponsors

Direct Donations

Please make checks out to “The Katie Lynch Foundation” and mail to:

  • The Katie Lynch Foundation
  • C/O Ascent Venture Partners
  • 255 State Street, 5th Floor
  • Boston, MA 02109

If you are sponsoring a runner, please write "sponsoring [runners name]" on the memo section of your check.

Charity Runners Raffle

To donate an item to the Charity Runners Raffle please contact charityrunners@katiesraces.com

To Donate By Credit Card

  • Visit http://www.active.com/donate/katiesraces2013
  • Use the search function at the top of the page if you wish to find an athlete to support.
  • Please contact Ashley at ashley@katiesraces.com with any questions.
