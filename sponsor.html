There are several ways to contribute to The Katie Lynch Foundation. Your support makes a difference and is greatly appreciated!

TO DONATE BY CHECK

Please make checks out to “The Katie Lynch Foundation” and mail to:

The Katie Lynch Foundation

C/O Ascent Venture Partners

255 State Street, 5th Floor

Boston, MA 02109

If you are sponsoring a runner, please write "sponsoring [runners name]" on the memo section of your check.

  • Please contact Ashley at ashley@katiesraces.com with any questions.
